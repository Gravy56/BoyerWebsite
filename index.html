Our Heritage

Nearly 90 Years & Not One Shortcut.

Boyer Candy Company was founded in Altoona, Pennsylvania in 1936. From day one, the goal was simple: make the best candy in America using real chocolate and real ingredients. That hasn't changed. The machinery has been upgraded. The recipes haven't.

We're still in Altoona. We're still making Mallo Cups. And we're still doing it the right way.
