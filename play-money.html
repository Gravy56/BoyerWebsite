Step-by-Step

How to Redeem

  1. Gather Your Play Money Cards

    Collect all the Play Money cards from your Boyer candy purchases. Count up the total face value printed on each card. The minimum redemption is $1.00 in cards.

  2. Fill Out the Redemption Form

    Scroll down to the Redemption Form and enter your name, mailing address, email, and total Play Money value. Submit the form to begin your request.

  3. Mail In Your Physical Cards (If Required)

    After we receive your form, we may ask you to mail the original physical cards to our Altoona address for verification. We'll send instructions to the email you provided.

  4. Receive Your Reward

    Once your cards are verified, we'll send your reward directly to your mailing address. Please allow 4–6 weeks for processing and delivery.

Common Questions

Play Money FAQ

Play Money cards are included inside the packaging of most Boyer products — Mallo Cups, Clark Bars, Smoothie Cups, Peanut Butter Cups, and more. Each 2-pack wrapper typically contains one or more cards.

The minimum redemption is $1.00 in Play Money card value. There's no maximum — the more you save, the bigger the reward you can redeem for.

Boyer Play Money cards do not have an expiration date. Many collectors have been saving their cards for years or even decades. Older cards are still valid for redemption.

Play Money can be redeemed for free Boyer candy products. The specific rewards available depend on your total accumulated value. Submit the form above and we'll confirm your redemption options.
