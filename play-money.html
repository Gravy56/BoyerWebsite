Common Questions

Play Money FAQ

Where do I find the Play Money cards?

Play Money cards are included inside the packaging of most Boyer products — Mallo Cups, Clark Bars, Smoothie Cups, Peanut Butter Cups, and more. Each 2-pack wrapper typically contains one or more cards.

How many points do I need to redeem?

The minimum is 400 points for a magnet. Rewards go up to 1,500 points for a mug. See the full rewards menu above for all tiers.

Do the Play Money cards expire?

Boyer Play Money cards do not have an expiration date. Older cards are still valid. Note that Boyer reserves the right not to accept previous order forms, so use the current redemption sheet when you mail in.

What can I redeem my Play Money for?

There are 5 rewards: a Mallo Cup or Clark Bar magnet (400 pts), a $2 rebate check (500 pts), a 6-piece box of candy — ships Nov through Mar only (1,000 pts), a T-shirt in white or yellow (1,250 pts), and a Mallo Cup or Clark Bar mug (1,500 pts).

Do I mail in the cards or just fill out the form online?

You must mail in the physical cards. Print the redemption form, complete it, and send it together with your Play Money cards to: Boyer Candy Company, 821 17th Street, Altoona, PA 16601. Online forms alone are not accepted.

How long does redemption take?

Allow 10–12 weeks for processing after Boyer receives your mailed form and cards. Boyer is not responsible for lost or damaged mail, so consider using a trackable mail service.
