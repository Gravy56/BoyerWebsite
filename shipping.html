Shipping Policy

Free standard shipping on all online orders of $75 or more. Ships from Altoona, Pennsylvania.
Overview

How We Ship

All Boyer Candy orders are processed and shipped from our factory in Altoona, Pennsylvania. We ship via standard carriers including USPS, UPS, and FedEx depending on your location and selected shipping method.

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days of being placed. You will receive a tracking number via email once your order has shipped.

Rates & Timeframes

Shipping Rates

Order Value Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
$75.00 and over Standard Shipping 5–7 Business Days FREE
Under $75.00 Standard Shipping 5–7 Business Days Calculated at checkout
Any order Expedited Shipping 2–3 Business Days Calculated at checkout

Delivery estimates are from the date of shipment, not the date of order. Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays.

Warm Weather Shipping

Heat Advisory

⚠ Important: Chocolate Melting Risk

Boyer Candy contains real chocolate, which can melt in high temperatures. During warm months (typically May through September), we strongly recommend selecting expedited shipping to minimize time in transit. Boyer Candy Company is not responsible for melted candy caused by high ambient temperatures during shipping.

If you're concerned about heat during transit, please select a faster shipping option at checkout. We ship Monday through Wednesday during summer months when possible to avoid packages sitting in hot facilities over weekends.

Delivery Issues

Lost, Damaged, or Missing Orders

If your order arrives damaged or doesn't arrive at all, please contact us within 14 days of the expected delivery date. Include your order number and, for damaged orders, a photo of the condition in which the package arrived.

We'll work with you to make it right — whether that's a replacement shipment or a refund, depending on the situation.

Domestic Only

Where We Ship

Boyer Candy currently ships to all 48 contiguous United States. We do not currently ship to Alaska, Hawaii, US territories, or internationally. We're working on it.
